V. COLLECTIVENESS OF FACTIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
It's important to ensure that factions work together and raise the alarm.
Raising the alarm - upon seeing a player or enemy at the base, a faction NPC will raise the alarm.
Teamwork - when fighting a player or enemy, factions will work together as a team rather than scattered.
During combat, melee soldiers will protect against attempts to kill ranged soldiers or mages.
NPC allies will cover them when their HP drops below 35%.
This will give NPCs more vigorous defense of their base or during an attack on another's base.

------------------------------------------------------------
VI. NPC INTERACTIONS AND REGULAR LIFE
------------------------------------------------------------
Currently, the life of any NPC consists of sleeping, walking, and standing still.
I think players would appreciate more variety in NPC life.
It's possible to temporarily change NPC life after an attack on their base.
During normal times, NPCs typically perform their routine.
They eat, cook, talk, stand guard, sit, play (this part mostly applies to children),
and perform actions related to specific Factions.
After an attack or battle, NPCs can treat wounds,
bury the dead, or hold meetings (this could be added to normal times).
NPC relationships could be enhanced with friendship (certain NPCs are more likely to be around each other or talk to each other)
or gifts.
